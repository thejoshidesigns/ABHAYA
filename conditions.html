What we help with

A wide range of mental health conditions.

ADHD

Attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder affects focus, impulse control, and the ability to regulate attention in daily life. We provide thorough evaluation for adolescents and adults, with treatment that may include medication, behavioral strategies, and supportive therapy.

See treatment options

Anxiety

Anxiety is more than occasional worry. It can be persistent, physical, and exhausting. We work with generalized anxiety, social anxiety, and anxiety that shows up in the body, helping you understand what's driving it and giving you tools that actually fit your life.

See treatment options

Panic disorder

Panic attacks can feel terrifying and unpredictable. With the right care, you can learn to recognize what's happening in your body, reduce the frequency of attacks, and rebuild confidence in daily life.

See treatment options
Commonly treated

Depression

Depression can make even small things feel impossibly heavy. Whether you're experiencing a first episode or have been navigating depression for years, we offer thoughtful evaluation and treatment that honors your whole story, not just the symptoms.

See treatment options

Bipolar disorder

Bipolar disorder involves shifts in mood, energy, and activity that go well beyond normal ups and downs. With careful diagnosis and steady, long-term care, most people find real stability.

See treatment options

PTSD

Post-traumatic stress disorder is the body's continued response to something that has already ended. We offer trauma-informed care at a pace that feels safe to you, with treatments proven to help.

See treatment options

OCD

Obsessive-compulsive disorder is more than being particular. It's intrusive thoughts and compulsions that take up real time and energy. We offer skilled, evidence-based care that respects what you're going through.

See treatment options

Borderline personality disorder

BPD is a deeply valid pattern of intense emotion, relationship experiences, and identity questions, and it responds well to the right kind of consistent care. Our approach is warm, structured, and grounded in evidence-based therapies.

See treatment options

Schizophrenia

Schizophrenia and related conditions deserve careful, compassionate, and expert psychiatric care. We provide long-term medication management and supportive therapy with a focus on stability, recovery, and quality of life.

See treatment options

Eating disorders

Eating disorders are serious conditions that affect the body, mind, and relationships with food. We provide psychiatric evaluation and ongoing care, and coordinate closely with dietitians and higher levels of care when needed.

See treatment options

Substance use

Substance use exists on a wide spectrum, and so does care. Whether you're exploring your relationship with alcohol, navigating recovery, or somewhere in between, we offer nonjudgmental support and evidence-based treatment.

See treatment options

Grief & bereavement

Grief doesn't follow a timeline. Whether your loss is recent or long ago, we offer a space to process what's happened without rushing you toward "moving on." Healing happens when grief is honored, not minimized.

A consistent approach

Whatever the diagnosis, the same fundamentals apply

The specific plan depends on you, but every condition we treat is approached with the same commitments.

  1. 01

    Your story, first

    A thorough evaluation focused on what's actually happening for you, not just a checklist of symptoms. Time to ask questions, time to be heard.

  2. 02

    Clear recommendations

    A plan you understand: what we're recommending, why, what to expect, and what we'd try next if this doesn't work. Decisions made together, in plain language.

  3. 03

    Steady follow-up

    Ongoing care that adjusts as you do, through better periods and harder ones. We keep showing up, and we keep adjusting the plan.
