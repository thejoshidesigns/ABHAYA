Meet the provider

Dr. Swarnatha Yerrapu, MD

Dr. Yerrapu is a psychiatrist serving adolescents and adults in central Missouri. Her practice is small on purpose, so that every patient gets time, attention, and a treatment plan that fits their actual life.

She trained in general psychiatry and brings experience across inpatient, outpatient, and consultation-liaison settings. Her clinical interests include mood and anxiety disorders, ADHD across the lifespan, trauma-informed care, and the thoughtful use of medication as part of a broader plan that always includes therapy, lifestyle, and the patient's own goals.

Outside the office, she is known for taking the time to explain. Not just what a medication does, but why it might be worth considering, what to expect, and when to question it. That same patience shapes every part of the practice.

How we work

Three things that shape every visit

These are the commitments behind every recommendation, every prescription, and every conversation in this practice.

01

Personalized, never generic

No two patients are alike, and no two treatment plans should be either. We start with your story, what you've tried, what's worked, what hasn't, and what matters to you, and we build from there.

02

Evidence with a human face

We use what research supports, and we're honest about what it doesn't. Medications, therapy, lifestyle change, each tool has its place, and we talk through the trade-offs out loud together.

03

Care that fits real life

Columbia-based care plus secure telepsychiatry across Missouri. Most major insurance accepted, transparent self-pay rates, and responsive scheduling when possible.
