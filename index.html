How it works

Three steps to get started.

Whether you need one service or all three, the path looks the same, and it starts when you're ready.

"The first call was the hardest. After that, everything got a little easier." A note from a patient, with permission

  1. 01

    Request an appointment

    Fill out our brief intake form, or call us. We'll confirm details and answer questions before your first visit.

  2. 02

    First visit

    A 60–75 minute evaluation where we get to know you, your history, and your goals. No pressure, no script.

  3. 03

    Your plan

    We talk through recommendations together. Therapy, medication, lifestyle, or a combination, and we build a plan that fits your life.

About the practice

Meet Dr. Swarnatha Yerrapu.

Abhaya means fearless, without fear in Sanskrit. The name is a quiet reminder that healing often starts when we feel safe enough to be honest, and that the relationship between patient and provider is the foundation of that safety.

Dr. Yerrapu is a board-certified psychiatrist providing personalized care for adolescents and adults across Missouri. The practice combines thoughtful medication management with evidence-based therapy, in a setting designed to feel more like a conversation than an appointment.
