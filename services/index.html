What is Spravato®?

An FDA-approved nasal spray for adults with treatment-resistant depression, administered in-office under medical supervision per the FDA's REMS program. Each dose is monitored on-site for at least two hours.

Get notified when available

Who it's for

Adults (18+) with major depressive disorder who have tried at least two antidepressants without adequate relief and can commit to in-office visits with post-dose monitoring.

What to expect

Initial consultation, supervised on-site administration (twice weekly at first, then weekly), two-hour monitored recovery after each dose, and ongoing coordination with your existing plan.

How it works

Three steps to get started

Whether you need one service or all three, the path looks the same.

  1. 01

    Request an appointment

    Fill out our brief intake form, or call us. We'll confirm details and answer questions before your first visit.

  2. 02

    First visit

    A 60–75 minute evaluation where we get to know you, your history, and your goals. No pressure, no script.

  3. 03

    Your plan

    We talk through recommendations together - therapy, medication, lifestyle, or a combination - and build a plan that fits your life.
