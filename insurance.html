Before your first visit

How to verify your coverage

A few minutes of prep on your end can prevent surprise bills later. Here's what to ask your insurance company when you call the member services number on the back of your card.

  1. 1

    Confirm we're in-network

    Ask: "Is Abhaya Behavioral Health in-network for outpatient mental health?" If a specific clinician is involved, use their full name and NPI.

  2. 2

    Ask about your benefits

    Get clarity on your copay or coinsurance, your deductible status, and any visit limits. Specifically ask about both psychiatry (90834, 90837, 99213, 99214) and therapy codes.

  3. 3

    Check if prior authorization is needed

    Some plans require pre-approval before the first visit. If so, ask what they need from us and how long it takes.

  4. 4

    Save the reference number

    Write down the rep's name, the call reference number, and the date. If a billing question comes up later, this protects you.

Common questions

Quick answers about insurance

Do you bill insurance directly?

Yes. For the plans we are in-network with, we bill your insurance and you are responsible only for your copay, coinsurance, or deductible at the time of service.

What if my plan is out-of-network?

You can still see us. We can provide a superbill for you to submit to your insurance for possible out-of-network reimbursement. Self-pay rates also apply.

Do you accept Medicaid or Medicare?

Yes, both Missouri Medicaid and Medicare are listed among our accepted plans. Coverage details depend on your specific plan.

Can I use my HSA or FSA card?

Yes. Mental health care is an eligible HSA/FSA expense. We accept HSA/FSA cards as well as all major credit cards.
